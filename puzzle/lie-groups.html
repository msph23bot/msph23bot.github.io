MATH 200 Lie Groups

These groups of cells contain 9 lies.

  • There is one lie in each row and in each column.
  • Each digit from 1-9 will lie once; the lies themselves may be either inside or outside the 1-9 range, but they are always positive integers.
  • None of the clues contain lies, but a cell with a clue may contain a lie.

The grid below is solvable online. No validation will be performed.

  • Place a digit in each cell by typing 1-9. Candidates may be marked by holding Shift when typing a digit.
  • Press L in a cell to toggle it as a lie. Press T in a cell to toggle it as truth.
  • The result of a cell’s lie can be recorded by holding Ctrl when typing one or more digits. You can use Ctrl+Space, Ctrl+Backspace, etc. to clear.
