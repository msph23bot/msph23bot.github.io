The Music of Rascal Flatts

WARNING: SUGGESTIVE LYRICS

This puzzle contains original lyrics presented in “radio edit” form, with a portion bleeped out of the audio and captions. The bleeped portion will bring to mind swear words and words of a sexual nature. You won’t need the bleeped lyrics at all in order to solve the puzzle; they are merely intended to be humorous, and do not reference any individuals or groups.

If nobody on your team is comfortable with this, then an alternative task can be assigned to your team. Send us mail at msph2023@gmail.com to request an alternative.

If you’re SURE you’d like to continue, click the button below.


These rascally flats (defined here) have mixed themselves up!

  • Each flat is presented as parody lyrics for a song, ending with a bleep.
  • For each song, choose one of the transformations below to transform the title of a different song parodied in this puzzle into a word that can replace the bleep in this song.
  • When solving flats, an answer word does not generally rhyme with its cueword; in fact, you won’t need anything from the original bleeped words to solve this puzzle.
  • Each song title will be used once, each set of lyrics will be used once, and each transformation will be used once.

For definitions and examples of these flat types, see https://enigma.puzzlers.org/guide/browse, or click the links below.

  • DELETION (6)
  • FIFTH-LETTER CHANGE (6)
  • FIFTH-TO-FIRST CHANGEOVER (2)
