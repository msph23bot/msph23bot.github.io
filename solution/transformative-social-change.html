Solution: Transformative Social Change
Answer: WRACK ZONE

Written by Jonah Ostroff

Each puzzle in the Sociology department uses parts of the profiles of two fish. This puzzle uses the word search from Octopus’s Chumblr page, and the “Goals” section of Salmon’s PokeCupid page.

As clued by the text on Octopus’s page, the rows in the word search are all what3words addresses. Each one lies in a body of water:

Order Address Body of water

9 landed.comically.donate Pence Lake

The Salmon goals are arranged into three sentences of three, so the next step is to look up these nine words on what3words, in three sets of three:

These words can be transformed again with the second paragraph:

Original Goal New Explanation

DORA Get a monkey. BOOTS Dora the Explorer’s monkey is named Boots.

copper.grow.boots is not in a body of water, but we can follow the last instruction anyway: go one space to the southeast to find the square shift.middle.twice. The word search grid is 21 squares wide, and Caesar shifting the middle column (UPYAIXMLC) by two gives the answer: WRACK ZONE.
