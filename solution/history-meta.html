Solution: History Meta
Answer: A CLERICAL ERA

Written by Amos Eshel and Jason Deakins

Each of the answers in this department reads like a newspaper headline and references a particular historic event...twice! These dates can be used to identify particular letters in the given calendar which looks like a month, but is extended further to be capable of handling a month that starts on any day of the week and goes as long as 31 days.

As an example, May 2023 begins on a Monday; May 7, 2023 therefore is the Sunday in the 2nd row, an A. Extracting two letters per answer and ordering them by when they occurred (which ends up just being by year, since no two events happened in the same year), yields the answer A CLERICAL ERA.
