Solution: The British Invasion
Answer: DECK ROOF

Written by Kenny Young

In this puzzle, “the British Invasion” is the letter U, which gets itself into words like “honoured”. Each of these albums includes a track with a word that can have a letter U added to it to make a word matching one of the clues at the bottom of the puzzle. Many of these artists have multiple songs that have this property, but the specific albums provided have only one.

Artist Track Clue New word
James Taylor You’ve Got a Friend Disease GOUT
John Barry James Bond Theme Jump BOUND
Paula Abdul Cold Hearted Auxiliary verb COULD
Steely Dan Rikki Don’t Lose That Number Insect LOUSE
Talking Heads Once in a Lifetime Unit of weight OUNCE
Billy Joel Big Shot Loud noise SHOUT
Lionel Richie Penny Lover One of many in a window treatment LOUVER
Poison Every Rose has its Thorn Wake up ROUSE

From here, you can shift the first letter of each new word by the provided amount. [It’s also reasonable to try shifting the track numbers, but the -12 for the Poison track goes negative and knocks out that approach.]

New word Shift Letter
GOUT -3 D
BOUND +3 E
COULD +0 C
LOUSE -1 K
OUNCE +3 R
SHOUT -4 O
LOUVER +3 O
ROUSE -12 F

This spells out the answer DECK ROOF.
