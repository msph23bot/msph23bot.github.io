Solution: PH22: Blue Moons
Answer: MELANCHOLY

Written by Dana Young

DARK WORLD

The Dark World solves to this mask, which will be applied to the other two grids.

JURASSIC WORLD

Find NATO phonetic letters in this word search.

The leftover letters spell FIFTH LETTERS.

Every fifth letter on the masked Jurassic World grid spells: MELON

BINARY WORLD

Connect each clue on the left with one on the right by adding “And” or “Or” in the middle.

The crossed letters spell COUNT BY PI.

Counting visible letters (ignoring blanks) by digits of pi on masked Binary World spells: COLLIE

FEELING BLUE

Combine both MELON and COLLIE to get the answer, MELANCHOLY.
