Solution: MATH 15 Category Theory
Answer: SESAME CHICKEN

Written by Justin Melvin

This puzzle refers to the excellent British quiz show Only Connect. Only Connect consists of four rounds:
  1. Connections (find the category that connects 4 clues)
  2. Sequences (given a sequence of three items, find the fourth in the sequence)
  3. Connecting Wall (given 16 items, sort them into four categories of four items and identify the categories)
  4. Missing Vowels (given a category and a member of that category with vowels removed, identify that member, e.g. NLY CNNCT->ONLY CONNECT)
The first step is to partition the interactive walls into groups of four (which are confirmed when they are found) and identify the categories:

The 'Round 2' extraction is CHNSDSHS, which is a 'Round 4' missing vowels phrase.
The 'Round 1' sets are BOOK / QUESTION / SECRET / HOUSE (can be preceeded by 'Open') and SCARED / SPINELESS / YELLOW / CRAVEN (synonyms for each other).
The 'Round 2' answer can be re-vowelated to CHINESE DISHES, the category for the final answer.
The 'Round 1' ones, "can be preceeded by 'open' " + "synonym for scared" can be answered by
