Solution: Historic Preservation
Answer: BAKER STARTS PLAYING DOCTOR

Written by Jonah Ostroff

The puzzle consists of 24 things to find, followed by 24 panoramas, so the first thing to do is to try to find them. Each clued business appears in one panorama, and the panoramas are arranged alphabetically by the names of the businesses. Each panorama also comes with a number, which can be indexed into the names:

Author’s Notes

Most of the former Pizza Huts in this puzzle were found on /r/FormerPizzaHuts.

Originally, the plan was to embed a navigable Google Street View of each location, but many of the FPHs turn over often enough that moving to a nearby Street View location (taken at a different time) can reveal a different business. I was also dreading having to fact-check the puzzle right up to the day of the hunt.
