Solution: The Middle Class
Answer: BUSINESS

Written by Jonah Ostroff

Each puzzle in the Sociology department uses parts of the profiles of two fish. This puzzle uses the photos from Salmon’s PokeCupid page, and ten clips from Tuna’s Codify page.

There are five artists who each have exactly two clips on Tuna’s playlist, and their names start with A-E: Aerosmith, Beyoncé, Coldplay, Daft Punk, and Ed Sheeran.

Each of these artists has one clip from the end of a song, and one clip from the beginning of a song. As clued by the title, we should find the middle somehow. These songs are always two tracks apart on an album, so the middle is the song in between:

Beyoncé Cuff It Energy Break My Soul
Coldplay A Head Full of Dreams Birds Hymn for the Weekend
Daft Punk Get Lucky Beyond Motherboard
Ed Sheeran Shape of You Perfect Galway Girl

The Salmon pictures are presented in alphabetical order. Five of them have a Salmon on the left, and five have a Salmon on the right. These pictures can be paired to create five before-and-afters, where the middle word (replacing the Salmon) is the name of one of the middle songs:
