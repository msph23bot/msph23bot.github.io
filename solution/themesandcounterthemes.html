Solution: Themes and Counterthemes
Answer: PARIS, SHELF

Written by Justin Melvin

First, solvers should notice that each column is in alphabetical order. By taking one letter from each column, one can form four items that are in a category. The leftover letters are one letter shy of another member of the category.

Taking the letters to insert gives the first answer (the 'theme'): PARIS.

To find the 'countertheme', note that there is another letter that could be uniquely added (and in the same location) to each leftover to make a non-thematic word:

NOUGhT
CONGEe

fIESTA

These spell the counter-theme: SHELF.
