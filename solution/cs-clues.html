We then combine the words on the right with the mastermind results, and try to find a word that fits those constraints.

This means we are looking for a word that shares 1 letter not in the correct position with FLUE, 2 letters not in the correct position with THIS, ..., 1 letter not in the correct position with [BORN | FIRM | LOCK | ROLE], ...

The blank for the input is on the right, which is why we get four different results.

Author’s Notes

The exact mechanics of this puzzle were figured out pretty late in the process (using Mastermind was the driving force from the start). This is unfortunate as I think if CASE had been replaced by input, a lot more freedom would have existed for the input/output pairs. As it is, the original idea of this puzzle acting on 7 letter words (and providing CONTROL to the final meta phrase) had to be abandoned for something constructible at 4 letters, and this was by far the most constrained construction of the 3 content-filled puzzles.
