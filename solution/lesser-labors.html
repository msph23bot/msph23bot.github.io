This comprises all seventy competitors from the first 14 series of the show. Solvers will have likely noticed that the list is alphabetized by competitors’ first name—this is a canonical ordering on the show as well, with the five competitors always sitting in this order left-to-right. Re-sorting the scores per series thus gives a sequence of five-bit binary that converts to GO SUBMIT BENDER.
