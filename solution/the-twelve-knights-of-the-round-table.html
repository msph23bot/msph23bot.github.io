Solution: The Twelve Knights of the Round Table
Answer: COLTS WIN THE SUPER BOWL

Written by Kenny Young

There are many tales of the Knights of the Round Table and many lists thereof, but there is a canonical list of the core 12 (modulo spelling):

  • Sir Bedivere
  • Sir Bors the Younger
  • Sir Gaheris
  • Sir Galahad
  • Sir Gareth
  • Sir Gawain
  • Sir Geraint
  • Sir Kay
  • Sir Lamorak
  • Sir Lancelot
  • Sir Percival
  • Sir Tristan
