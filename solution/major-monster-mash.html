This was a group effort, many thanks to:

  • Kenny Young for the front-end code.

  • Dana Young, Kenny Young, & Jonah Ostroff for help connecting some of the tougher answers to the graph.

    • The meta-meta is a funny farm that uses the event's meta answers as the initial nodes. The goal is to make connections to all the individual puzzle answers from the entire event. When answers to puzzles that team's have solved are found in the graph they are colored red. Answer nodes are grouped in sets of 5. Finding some red nodes in a group will reveal a letter in the final answer; finding all 5 in a group will provide the letter after the previous one.
